About me

My name is Abhishek, and I'm excited to share my story with you. Hailing from the beautiful state of Bihar, I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering at the prestigious IIT Bombay.

My educational journey began at Chapra Central School in Chhapra Saran, where I completed my matriculation. I then went on to pursue my Intermediate studies at H.D. Jain College in Ara, Bhojpur.

Although my chosen field of study is civil engineering, I have a profound interest in programming and coding. The world of technology never fails to captivate me, and I constantly find myself exploring new avenues in this exciting domain.

Beyond academics and engineering, I am a person with a deep passion for creativity and expression. Sketching is a treasured hobby of mine, allowing me to bring my imagination to life on paper. Additionally, I find solace in playing the guitar. The harmonious melodies that resonate from the strings offer a form of relaxation and self-expression that words often fail to convey.
